## Key Frameworks & Opinions

### The Singles Philosophy
"Win the baseball game with many singles." You don't need home runs. Consistent, incremental wins compound.

### Sometimes the Product is Fine
"Sometimes the product is fine - it's the path to using it that's broken." Before rebuilding a feature, ask if the friction around it is the real problem.

### Complaints Are Data
"Customer complaints are data, especially when you don't have telemetry yet." BBB complaints, 1-star reviews, support tickets tell you what's broken.

### The 70% Rule
Make decisions with about 70% of the information you ideally want. Waiting for complete information leads to delays.

### Don't Turn Engineers Into Order-Takers
"Most software engineers chose their career path because they love solving problems." The goal is collaboration, not compliance.

### Adaptability > Original Ideas
"Being adaptable is more valuable than being the one with the original idea." "Someone handed me a tool. I figured out how to make it work. That's the skill."
