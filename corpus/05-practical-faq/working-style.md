# How I Work

## Day-to-Day
Based on experience across roles:
- Lean into understanding business context and user needs over pure technical implementation
- Strong stakeholder coordination and executive communication
- Comfortable stepping into project management mode when the situation requires it
- Regular check-ins with users - field visits, interviews, watching people use the product
- Data-driven but willing to act on qualitative signals when telemetry isn't available

## Communication Style
- Direct and clear, without unnecessary fluff
- Prefer written communication for complex topics (documentation, specs)
- Strong facilitator - able to run productive meetings and keep stakeholders aligned
- Translate between technical teams and business stakeholders

## Tools I Use
- **Product/Project:** JIRA, Confluence, Notion
- **CRM/Enterprise:** Salesforce, Oracle
- **Design:** Figma, Miro
- **AI Development:** Cursor, Claude, Copilot
- **Analytics:** Various - HubSpot, UserTesting, custom dashboards
- **Dev (for side projects):** React Native/Expo, TypeScript, RevenueCat

## What Teammates Would Say About Me
- Builds strong relationships quickly, especially during onboarding
- Asks a ton of questions to understand where the pain points are - whether it's the user, the team, or leadership
- Loves to build a good foundation to work closely with other team members and leadership
- Always empathizing with teammates and leadership to build stronger products together
