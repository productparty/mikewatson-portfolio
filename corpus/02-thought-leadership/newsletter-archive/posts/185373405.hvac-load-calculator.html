Common Questions

How do I choose which calculation mode?
Start with Moderate for most jobs. Use Simple when speed matters more than precision. Use Comprehensive when you need maximum accuracy.

Where is my project data stored?
All projects save automatically to your iPhone. Your data stays on your device - no cloud uploads.

Can I use this offline?
Yes! The app works completely offline. All calculations and climate data are built in.

What if my ZIP code isn’t in the database?
The app uses smart fallback logic (same area → same region → US averages). You can also manually enter custom degree day values.

Is there a limit on projects?
No limits. Create as many projects as you want.

What iOS version do I need?
iOS 15 or later. Works on iPhone and iPad.
